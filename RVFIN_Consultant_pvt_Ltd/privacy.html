Last updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

RVFIN Consultants Pvt Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023 and other applicable laws.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone number, address
  • Professional Details: Company, job title, qualifications
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage Data: Website interaction metrics

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us:

  • Provide consultancy services
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Improve our website
  • Comply with legal obligations

We never sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We may share data with:

  • Regulatory authorities (as required by law)

5. Your Rights

Under DPDP Act 2023, you can:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request deletion (subject to legal requirements)

Email requests to: privacy@rvfin.com

  • Role-based access controls
